Community Air Quality Insights

Analysis and takeaways from Cheverly’s local sensor network

What are the health effects?

Fine particulate matter (PM2.5) consists of tiny particles that can reach deep into the lungs and even the bloodstream. For sensitive groups—including children, seniors, and those with respiratory conditions—elevated levels can trigger asthma attacks or other health issues.

About the community network

Our network uses a mix of PurpleAir and QuantAQ sensors. These devices measure light scattering to estimate particle counts in real-time. By comparing local data to national trends, we can identify specific times of day when air quality might require extra caution.

Who’s taking action?

The Town of Cheverly is committed to environmental justice and transparent data. Residents can take part by monitoring the dashboard before planning outdoor activities and supporting local initiatives to reduce vehicle idling and wood burning.
