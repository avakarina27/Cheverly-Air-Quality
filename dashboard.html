Cumulative Exposure Index (CE-AQI)

Our network tracks PM2.5, Black Carbon, Nitrogen Dioxide, and Ozone. The CE-AQI combines these into a single score.

Base Value: The highest individual pollutant score.
Adders: Penalties added when multiple pollutants are elevated at once.
