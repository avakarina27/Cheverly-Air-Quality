Why Cumulative Exposure Matters

Traditional air quality reports often focus on a single pollutant. However, Cheverly residents are often exposed to a mix of diesel exhaust, industrial dust, and fine particulate matter (PM2.5) simultaneously.

Our CE-AQI (Cumulative Exposure Air Quality Index) is a creative tool that uses solid science to communicate the real-world impact of breathing multiple pollutants at once.
