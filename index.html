Why Cumulative Exposure Matters

Traditional maps often only show one pollutant at a time. Our CE-AQI (Cumulative Exposure Air Quality Index) combines PM2.5 and Black Carbon to show the true combined health impact.

By factoring in localized environmental data, we ensure that community members can make safe choices for their families.
