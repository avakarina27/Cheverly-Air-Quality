Why Cumulative Exposure Matters

Traditional maps often only show one pollutant at a time. Our CE-AQI (Cumulative Exposure Air Quality Index) combines fine particulate matter (PM2.5) and Black Carbon (Diesel Particulate Matter) to show the true combined health impact.

By factoring in the high annual averages of Black Carbon in our region, we ensure that community members have the data they need to make safe choices for their families.
